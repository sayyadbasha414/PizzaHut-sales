# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze pizza sales performance, customer ordering behavior, and revenue trends. This dashboard provides business insights through KPIs, dynamic filtering, and interactive visualizations to support better decision-making.


# 📊 Project Overview

The Pizza Sales Dashboard helps restaurant managers and business stakeholders monitor overall sales performance, identify top-performing pizza categories, analyze customer ordering patterns, and track monthly revenue trends.

The dashboard provides an easy-to-understand executive summary with interactive filters for deeper analysis.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor total revenue and sales performance
- Analyze pizza category contribution
- Identify best-selling pizza sizes
- Track monthly revenue trends
- Understand customer ordering patterns
- Compare revenue and order volume
- Improve business decision making using data

# 💡 Business Insights

✔ Classic pizzas generated the highest revenue.

✔ Large-sized pizzas contributed the highest sales.

✔ Afternoon and evening time slots received the maximum orders.

✔ Revenue remained consistent throughout the year with noticeable seasonal peaks.

✔ Interactive filtering enables detailed analysis by month, category, and pizza type.
